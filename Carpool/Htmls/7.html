Contacting Other Drivers
 
You may contact other drivers as a group or individually in two ways. In the following scenario, weâ€™ll use a soccer camp carpool as an example.
 
Option 1:
 
1) Tap any event block for the Soccer Camp event. (you will be brought to the Event Details page)
 
2) Tap the chat icon on the top of the screen
 
Option 2:
 
1) Tap the chat icon on the bottom menu
 
2) One the following page, choose from the list of carpool you would like to contact
 
Once you have done either of the above option, you will be taken to the Message Page. You may choose to text or email one or more carpool members by tapping their name. Tap their name again to deselect them. A check mark will appear next to the selected names.
 
To contact a driver by phone, tap the phone icon to the left of their name to call their cell number.
