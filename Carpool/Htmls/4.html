Inviting Drivers to an Event
 
Once you have created an event, you will be given the option to invite other drivers to view and participate in the event. If the invited driver does not have the app, they will be sent a text message instructing them to download the app in order to participate in the event.
 
Invited drivers may:
 
1) View the event you have invited them to
 
2) Accept or Decline the invite
 
3) Add or remove their children
 
4) Choose driving assignments
