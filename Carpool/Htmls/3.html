Scheduling Events
 
Scheduling an Event
 
When you create a carpool event you are referred to as the Creator. If you are invited to join another person’s carpool event, you are referred to as an Invitee.
 
There are two ways to create of an event.
 
1) There are two ways to create of an event.
 
2) Tap the plus icon “+” at the top right corner of the Day View calendar.
 
3) Press and hold any open area on the day view calendar screen.
 
When choosing option 2 above, you may place the “Event Block” anywhere you want on the calendar. Lift your finger to set it in place and you will be taken to the Add Event page.
 
Begin creating your event by adding a title for your event. Next, enter the start and end time of the event.
 
Adding Passengers
 
Enter children by:
 
1) Tapping “Passengers”
 
2) On the following page, tap the passengers you want to include in the event on the pop up. (a check mark will appear next to the selected passenger’s name.)
 
3) Tap “Done” when you are finished adding passengers. Note: The other drivers that you invite will add or remove their own children to the event once you share it with them.
 
NOTE: If you did not add your children during the setup of the app, you will be prompted to do that here.
 
Creating a Repeating Event
 
To create a repeating event do the following
 
1) Tap “Repeats” on the Add Event page
 
2) On the following page you may choose the type of repeat you want.
 
3) After selecting the type of repeat, you will be prompted to choose an end date. Tap the end date and check marks will appear underneath the schedule events. If you need to change the end date, simply tap another end date.
 
If your event does not have an end date, tap the toggle switch Next to “use a specific end date”. This will create an ongoing repeating event with no end.
 
Once you are done scheduling your repeats, tap “Done” on the End Repeats page. You will be taken back to the Add Events page.
