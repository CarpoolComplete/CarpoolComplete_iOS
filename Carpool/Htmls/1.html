Getting Started
 
Initial Set Up
 
Set up the app by entering your name, cell phone, and email address. Next, choose your shared family password. Your shared family password will be used by the two adults in your family so don’t forget to share it with them.
 
Adding a Second Adult
 
When you first open the app and enter your information, you will automatically be registered as a family adult. Next, enter the information for a second adult in your household (i.e.: your spouse).
 
1) Tap "Add Adult 2"
 
2) Enter their name and email in the fields on the following page
 
3) Tap “Done”
 
• Each family adult must be over 18 years old.
 
• This adult may sign in to their account using this email address and the shared family password.
 
• Both family adults will be able to add and remove your children from carpools that you join.
 
Adding Children
 
To Add a Child:
 
1) Tap "Add a Child"
 
2) Enter their name in the fields on the following page
 
3) Tap “Done”
 
NOTE: You should only add children that live in your household to this list. (i.e.: your son/daughter). Your friends will add their children in their app.
 
Syncing Your Internal Calendar
 
You may choose to sync your carpool events with your phone’s internal calendar. By tapping “sync”, all of your carpool events will appear in your phone’s internal calendar.
