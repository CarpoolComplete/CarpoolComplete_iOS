Invites
 
Viewing Sent & Received Invites
 
To view invites that you have sent or received, tap the Invites icon.
 
When you receive an invite, the invites icon will appear with a number showing the number of invites you have yet to respond to.
 
The invites section displays invites that you have sent as well as those you have received. Tap “Sent” or “Received” at the top of the page to view each list. Tap Prev|Next to cycle through the list.
 
Sent Invites
 
Sent invites are sorted by event start date. The drivers you have invited appear underneath the event title. Their status (Accept or Decline) is displayed to the right of their name. If you have invited a driver and they have not downloaded the app yet, the word “Invited” will show next to their name.
 
Received Invites
 
Received invites are sorted by event start date. To respond to an invite, you can select one of two icons: Accept, or Decline. Note: Accepting an invite does not commit you to any driving assignments. Even if you have accepted an invitation to join a carpool, you may select to decline the invite later.
 
When You Accept a Carpool Invite, your calendar will show all the currently scheduled events for that carpool. You will have the ability to add your children and choose driving assignments for any of the events. The creator of the event may update and edit the event and these changes will be reflected in your carpool calendar.
 
When You Decline a Carpool Invite, the invite will be removed and no events will show up for that carpool. NOTE: If you accept an invite and later decide to decline the invite, although already scheduled events will remain in your calendar, future changes or edits to the carpool by the creator will not be reflected in your calendar.
