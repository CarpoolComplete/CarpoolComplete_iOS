Members Section
 
To get to the Members Section tap the members icon on the bottom menu
 
Viewing Carpools
 
To view you carpools tap on “Your Carpools”. Carpools are listed by start date. Underneath each title are the drivers that have accepted the invitation to join the carpool. Tap Prev|Next to cycle through the list of carpools.
 
Creators have the ability to invite or remove driver from this page. Tap “Invite Drivers” or “Remove Drivers” to do so.
 
Adding a Family Member
 
To add a family member, do the following:
 
1) Tap the members icon on the bottom menu
 
2) Tap the “+” Icon at the top right of the page
 
To Add a Second Adult:
 
1) Tap “Add Adult 2”
 
2) Enter their name and email in the fields on the following page
 
3) Tap “Done”
 
NOTE: Only the first family user may add a second adult to the family profile.
 
To Add a Child:
 
1) Tap “Add a Child”
 
2) Enter their name in the fields on the following page
 
3) Tap “Done”
 
Viewing/Editing a Members Information
 
Click on the members icon to view the members section
 
To view a particular member’s information, tap on their name.
 
• Passenger’s profiles may be edited or removed
 
• You may edit your own profile
 
• Although the second adult’s profile may not be edited, they may be removed.
