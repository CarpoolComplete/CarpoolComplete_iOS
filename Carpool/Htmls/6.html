Choosing a Driving Assignment
 
You may choose a driving assignment in two ways: 1) Directly from the event block on the day view calendar or 2) from the event details page.
 
Choosing a driving assignment from the Day View calendar. When viewing any event block in the day view calendar, tap one of the steering wheel icons to select the corresponding driving assignment. Your name will appear next to it. Tap the steering wheel again to remove yourself from the driving assignment.
 
The steering wheel at the top right of the event block represents the driving assignment going to the event. The steering wheel at the bottom right of the event block represents the driving assignment returning from the event.
 
Choosing a driving assignment from the Event Details page. The Event Details page lists the drivers and passengers to and from an event. If a driving assignment has not been selected, the words “Driver Needed” will appear in red. Tap the words “Driver Needed”, and your name will appear as the driver. Tap your name again to remove yourself from the driving assignment.
 
When you choose a driving assignment or remove yourself from a driving assignment, a notification will be sent to the other carpool members who have their push notifications turned on.
